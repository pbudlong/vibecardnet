## 2. Gateway Networks (Deposits & Withdrawals)

You can **withdraw** USDC from any of these networks into the Gateway.

**Why is this powerful?**
Once funds are in Gateway, you can **withdraw them to ANY supported chain instantly**.

- Deposit on **Arbitrum**.
- Pay for a resource on **Arc**.
- Seller withdraws earnings on **Base**.
